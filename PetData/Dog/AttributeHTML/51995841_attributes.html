-Name: Angel
-Nicknames: Angie, Angel-face
-Theme song: "Angels" by Robbie Williams
-Breed: Staffordshire Bull Terrier
-Sex: Female/spayed
-Key descriptors: Timid, Observant, Affectionate
-Affection level: Medium
-What we love most: Angel is a sweet girl with a gentle soul. Though she may be timid at first, she warms up quickly and shows her affectionate side to those she trusts. Her watchful and observant nature makes her a great companion for quiet evenings at home.
-What she loves most: Angel loves treats and spending time with her favorite people. She may be cautious around strangers, but once she gets to know you, she'll shower you with love and affection.
-Dogs: Angel prefers to be your only canine companion
-Kids: Adults only please

Angel may be shy, but she has so much love to give to the right family. She's looking for patient and understanding adopters who will give her the time and space she needs to feel comfortable in her new home. Angel would thrive in a calm and quiet household with no kids or other pets. If you're looking for a loyal and affectionate companion, come meet our Angel at the Seattle Animal Shelter!

To meet Angel, please download and fill out an adoption survey (found here) - http://www.seattle.gov/Documents/Departments/AnimalShelter/Dog%20Adoption%20Questionnaire_Fillable.pdf and email it to animalcare@seattle.gov -
